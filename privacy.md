# Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2, 2026

MyTimeClock does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

## Data Handling

- **All data is stored locally** on your device using an on-device SQLite database (Room). No data is sent to any external server.
- **Clock in/out times** and related time entries are stored only on your device.
- **Pay rate** and settings (pay period, theme preference) are stored only on your device.
- **No analytics or tracking** libraries are included in this app.
- **No personal information** is collected, logged, or shared.
- **Donation** functionality opens the Cash App or Play Store via an Intent — no payment data is handled or transmitted by this app.

## Third-Party Services

This app does not use any third-party services that receive data from the app. The only external links are:

- Cash App (opened via Intent for donations)
- Google Play Store (opened via Intent to install Cash App)

No personal identifiers or app data are included in these requests.

## Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on the project repository.
